Siberian Husky

First registered in Canada, They are remarkably adaptable and shouldn't be over-exercised on hot days as Huskies are more of a cold weather breed.

  • Scientific Name:: Canis Lupus Familiaris
  • Average Height:: 21-23 inches
  • Life Expectancy:: 12 - 15 years
  • Coat:: Thick double coat

Originated in Siberia, Siberian Huskies have a beautiful, thick coat that comes in a variety of colours and markings. Their blue or multi-colored eyes and striking facial masks only add to the appeal of this breed.
